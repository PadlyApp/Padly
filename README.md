# Padly

A trusted platform for students, interns, and early-career professionals to find housing and compatible roommates.

---

## **Project Vision**

The mission of Padly is to eliminate the stress and fragmentation of finding housing during pivotal career and life transitions. We are building a centralized, secure, and user-centric platform that connects a community of peers, landlords, and subletters, enabling them to transact with confidence and ease.

## **The Problem**

Relocating for an internship, a new job, or simply moving to a new city is a defining experience, but securing housing is often the most challenging part. The current landscape is a chaotic mix of platforms that were not designed for this specific need.

*   **Fragmentation:** The search is spread across multiple channels like Reddit, Discord, and Facebook Marketplace, requiring immense manual effort to track and vet opportunities.
*   **Lack of Trust:** It is difficult to verify the identity and legitimacy of potential roommates or landlords, creating significant safety and compatibility concerns.
*   **High Competition:** In major cities, the demand for affordable housing is intense, leaving individuals feeling rushed and pressured into making poor decisions.
*   **Poor Organization:** Coordinating with potential roommates, comparing listings, and managing communications is inefficient and overwhelming.

## **Our Solution: A Centralized & Trusted Ecosystem**

Padly will be a dedicated platform that streamlines the entire housing search process. It serves as a single point of contact for discovering listings, finding like-minded roommates, and communicating securely.

Our core value proposition is **trust**. By focusing on verification and building a community around professional and academic affiliations, we provide a safer and more reliable alternative to the open web.

---

## **Key Features**

### **1. Unified User Profiles**
A user's profile is their identity on the platform. It captures all the essential information needed to make a good housing or roommate decision.
*   **Core Information:** Name, bio, and a profile picture.
*   **Professional Context:** Current or upcoming company, school, and role (e.g., "Software Engineer at Google").
*   **Housing Needs:** Desired city, budget range, and ideal move-in date.
*   **Lifestyle Preferences:** Key details on cleanliness, social habits, noise levels, and more to ensure roommate compatibility.

### **2. Comprehensive Listings**
Listings can be created by landlords offering official rentals or by users subletting a spare room. Each listing is a rich source of information.
*   **Lease Flexibility:** Clearly state if a lease is for a **fixed-term** (e.g., a 4-month internship) or **open-ended** (e.g., a standard 12-month lease that becomes month-to-month).
*   **Details:** Price, location, description, high-quality photos, and available move-in date.
*   **Amenities:** A structured list of features (e.g., Wi-Fi, in-unit laundry, parking, pet-friendly).
*   **Powerful Filtering:** Users can filter listings by price, availability, lease type, location, and even by the company or school of other tenants.

### **3. Roommate Posts**
For users who have a place or are looking to team up to find one. This feature allows users to "advertise" themselves.
*   **Content:** Specifies the user's budget, desired move-in date, preferred neighborhoods, and a description of what they are looking for in a roommate and housing situation.

### **4. Verification System**
This is the cornerstone of building trust within the community.
*   **Email-Based Verification:** Users can verify their profile by confirming ownership of a school (`.edu`) or company email address. Verified profiles will be highlighted.
*   **Admin Verification:** A manual fallback for users who may not have a company email yet.
*   **Enhanced Visibility:** Verified users and their posts will be prioritized in search results to reward participation in the trust system.

### **5. Secure Messaging**
All communication happens directly within the app, removing the need to share personal contact information prematurely.
*   **Direct, One-on-One Chat:** Enables seamless conversation between potential roommates or between a tenant and a landlord.
*   **Safety Features:** Includes basic moderation and reporting tools to handle any inappropriate conduct.

## **User Roles and Abilities**

| Role | Description | Core Abilities |
|---|---|---|
| **Renter** | An individual looking for a place or roommates. | Create a profile, post roommate requests, search listings, message other users, and verify their identity. |
| **Host** | A provider of a rental space (room or entire unit). | Post and manage listings, communicate with potential tenants, and review user profiles. |
| **Admin** | A moderator responsible for platform health. | Manage user reports, approve manual verifications, and ensure content quality. |

## **Core User Journey**

To ensure we build an intuitive product, we must focus on this primary user flow:

1.  **Sign Up & Onboard:** A new user creates an account and is guided through setting up their profile with their role, company/school, budget, and move-in date.
2.  **Verify (Optional but Encouraged):** The user is prompted to verify their profile via their school or company email.
3.  **Explore:** The user browses housing listings or roommate posts, using filters to narrow down the options.
4.  **Post:** The user creates their own listing to sublet a room or a roommate post to find a match.
5.  **Connect:** The user initiates a conversation with another member via the direct messaging system to coordinate details.

## **Future Vision**

While the immediate goal is to perfect the housing search, Padly has the potential to become a broader community hub for students and professionals. Future phases could include features like local meetups, shared community guides, and integrated rental payment processing.
